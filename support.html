Support

Need a hand with one of our Confluence Cloud apps? Email us and a real person will help you sort it out. There's no ticket form to fill in and nothing to sign up for.

Email support

Or write to us directly at support@cloudscript.io.

What to include

To help us answer quickly, it's useful to tell us which app you're using (for example CloudScript.io Mermaid), what you were trying to do, and what happened instead. A screenshot or the diagram code often helps.

Response times

We're a small team and answer every message ourselves. We aim to reply within a couple of business days; complex issues may take a little longer to investigate.

Privacy

Support is handled entirely by email — we don't use a third-party help-desk, a web form, or any tracking on this page. We use the details in your email only to respond to your request. See the Mermaid privacy policy for how that app handles data.
