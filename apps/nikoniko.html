CloudScript.io NikoNiko Calendar

CloudScript.io NikoNiko Calendar is a Confluence Cloud app for tracking how a team is feeling over time. It brings the Niko-niko calendar, a simple Agile practice in which team members record their daily mood, onto a Confluence page, so a team can capture moods as they happen and look back over them later, which is particularly useful at retrospective time.

The app exists to answer a question that is otherwise hard to reconstruct after the fact, namely "how was I feeling on this day last week, or last sprint", by making each day's mood quick to record and easy to review across a whole sprint.

What it does

You add the NikoNiko macro to a Confluence page and configure it through the standard Confluence configuration panel. Team members then record their mood, and the calendar builds up a picture of the team's mood over the sprint. The main view shows the whole sprint's mood at a glance, and a range of themes is available so the calendar can be styled to suit the team.

Data and privacy

The app stores a single mood indicator per entry, a number from zero to five, linked to your Confluence accountId (an identifier Atlassian treats as safe to use for this purpose). It does not store other personally identifiable information. A Confluence administrator can request an extract of this data, or request that it be deleted.
